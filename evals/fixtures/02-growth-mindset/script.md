# 02-growth-mindset

A script designed to exhibit HELPFUL patterns — growth mindset, reframing, self-compassion, evidence-based thinking. False-positive guard: we should NOT detect heavy unhelpful patterns here.

**Question being answered:** *"What are you going to be celebrating a year from today?"*

---

## Read this aloud at a steady pace (~45–60 seconds)

> A year from today I'll be celebrating closing my seed round. I know I'm going to learn things this year I don't know yet, and that's part of the work. When I look at where I was a year ago compared to where I am now — the evidence is pretty clear that I figured out how to do this kind of thing before, and I can figure it out again. There's a version of this that didn't work, and I'd be okay either way. What I want is to look back on this year and say I made the choices that were aligned with who I'm becoming, not the choices that felt safe. I'm building the company I'd want to work at.

---

## Why this script exists

False-positive guard. The pipeline should:

- **NOT** flag impostor-syndrome, perfectionism, fear-of-failure as limiting beliefs (low strength)
- **DO** detect helpful patterns: growth-mindset ("learn things I don't know yet"), evidence-based ("the evidence is pretty clear"), self-compassion ("I'd be okay either way"), acceptance, reframing
- Hedge count should be LOW (mostly certainty markers: "I will", "I'm building", "I figured out")
- Sentiment should be positive
- Verbatim phrase candidates: "I'm building the company I'd want to work at", "the choices that were aligned"
